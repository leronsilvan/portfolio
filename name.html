About Me

Hello! I’m a passionate and driven data science enthusiast with a strong foundation in Python, machine learning, and real-world problem-solving. With a background in both theoretical knowledge and practical implementation, I specialize in turning raw data into meaningful insights and building intelligent systems that make a difference.

Over the past year, I’ve built multiple end-to-end projects ranging from regression-based prediction models (like car price prediction and savings forecasting) to classification models (like loan approval prediction), and even time series forecasting (like stock and retail sales predictions). Each project has helped me deepen my understanding of the full data science lifecycle — from data collection and preprocessing, to model training, evaluation, and deployment with tools like Streamlit.

I’m constantly learning and exploring new technologies, and I aim to blend analytical thinking with creativity.
